Bits from Cookbook

  • Do not land on DK
  • There is a low invincible bair that can cover all of DK's up B mixups
  • If you shine DK out of his up B to ledge, he won't have a jump

Neutral Game - Zuppy

Core Strategy

  • CAMP CAMP CAMP: Dash dance camp and laser him. DK literally can't approach. Worse than Falco
  • You can shield everything on reaction or beat the startup with nair or up tilt etc
  • Shield is fine because his stuff isn't safe or plus
  • Your main objective is to just not over extend and get grabbed. If you keep that in mind always you should win

Spacing & Approach

  • You only have to watch out for bair - just don't run into it
  • When DK is facing you or trying to take space that's when you can approach
  • Don't approach when your back is to him unless you want to call out his bair with run up CC or raw up smash (risky af though because unsafe on shield)

Defense

  • He has a good down-down grab so be careful for that
  • Running shine is super good for getting out of the corner
  • You can't shield pressure him especially from behind because of up-B
  • I like to do nair shine into full hop falling up air because they shield grab everything

Punish Game - Zuppy

Combo Game

  • You combo him pretty hard off of up tilt and up airs
  • Forward throw is also good for tech chasing

Throw Game

  • UP THROW IS NOT REAL: He will wiggle out up air you at low percent - just dash dance grab it
  • It works at higher percent though - I also like to up throw up tilt at low percent or just shark at higher percent
  • Always throw DK if you can throw him off ledge. It leads to a good kill edgeguard opportunity

Cargo Throw

  • Try to actually mash out of cargo and grabs at low percent
  • Makes the chaingrab way harder

DI & Survival

  • Try to DI to side platforms if you can. On FD smash DI the up airs up so the combo ends earlier

Edgeguarding - Zuppy

Edgeguard Strategy

  • For edgeguarding DK you always want to grab ledge as late as possible then do an up-B stall
  • If they drift in they get burned and then shined and if they drift out you just roll up and hog them

Approach Options

  • He can do short hop punch to approach sometimes too so watch out for that mixup as well
  • I forgot to talk about that for his approach options. Other than that he can only fair or stomp which are slow and risky if he misses
