Fox vs Samus

Dealing with CC, missiles, and platform control

← Back to Matchups

Concepts from Pipsqueak

  • Outside of being under top platform, we can punish up B if it is not edge canceled
  • We can hold up and away to DI and get out, then laser land and up smash
  • If up B is not a threat / gets her punished, she must wavedash (back) to leave shield
  • Drill in place is unlikely to work as a way of containing her

Dealing with CC

  • If you drill, it apparently should be approaching drill to deal with retreats
  • You can also nair in a way that hits in front of her, but lands behind her
  • Extremely well spaced bair is OK after 30, but still not exactly desirable
  • Down smash can be wavedash up smash punished
  • If you can, get running shield vs f tilt
  • Samus wants to up tilt on our shield
  • Running down tilt is strong, especially when down tilt up air kills
  • High / Early aerials lose to up B, but if we can DI up B, it isn't strong

Neutral & Approach

  • If they try to approach behind a missile, Nair the missile
  • Do not up smash samus at low %, she will tech it and down smash us
  • At low % it is better to grab than up smash, even though grab has no guaranteed follow up
  • After up throw, if they do an aerial or a bomb and we full hop into it, we lose
  • If we get a waveshine near edge, grabbing and throwing her off may be best
  • Running shine → full hop → Falling up air can technically work
  • At high % weak bair and nair can both lead to up smash or up air
  • If we grab ledge at nearly the same time, we can hold edge longer
  • Ledgedash → shield is pretty good since her grab is mid

Ideas from Billy

  • When it comes to acting on the second forward intention, there are options for feinting after the dash in
  • We want samus in the air, and we want to be grounded
  • Down tilt, grab, and waveshine → launcher are preferred ways to get her in the air
  • Down tilt is a good forward facing poke. Often pokes under shield
  • Lasering a lot is very strong in this matchup
  • Fox is clearly winning when samus is chasing fox
  • It is best to wavedash directly under samus to give access to up tilt or up air
  • Samus jumping is a cue to get to ground as fast and safely as possible
  • Wavedash up tilt and up smash both work vs F smash on shield
  • Even fully spaced, Bair can get CC D smashed. Just don't
  • The higher up samus bombs, the more time you have to get straight under her
  • Bair sharking can be strong, just to advance the situation

Cookbook: Neutral

Running Shield

  • An easy place to start when building a neutral vs her is with running shield. Most of her moves, besides spaced ftilt which you usually take space after blocking, can be reliably punished on reaction oos. This will force her to preemptive grab you, where everything else including jump is now unlocked
  • Once you've running shielded enough, the samus will try to hard call it out. If you do anything else you can just punish her on reaction
  • Run up shield is obnoxious, eventually you can preemptively attack if they just refuse to bite OR grab (which you can obviously punish too)

Using Platforms

  • It's hard to get safe dmg at low %, one way is to laser and use plats to get her to chase you. If she jumps on the plat and guesses wrong she gets tagged

Down Tilt

  • Dtilt knocks down on asdi down at 23%, cc at 63% all hitboxes (dtilt has 3 hitboxes, one sends straight up, one 80 degrees, the other at 70 degrees but this doesn't matter for knockdown %s)
  • She can no longer AT holding both sticks straight down 47-55% depending on the hitbox (90 degree one 47%, 70 degree 55%)
  • Meaning, if you're using it to contest wd back or shield poke it gets unlocked at these %s, with ASDI down being the more relevant %s as that's what she'll be doing when she's unable to truly be in crouch (in shield, wavedash lag, ftilt recovery etc)
  • KJH often mid air shines to reflect missiles as he's not stuck in lag on the ground, making him much harder to punish, plus DJ is much quicker than grounded jump as you have no jump squat and can quickly jump to a plat
  • If you go for a dtilt call out on movement/shield poke, it's kinda awkward to punish sometimes. Samus might be tempted to try and grab it, as you'll generally be shielding after most of your moves, which can be hard punished even out of a hard
  • Laser some to bait her to try and contest but do not nair in here, dtilt is a much better move to catch grounded movement due to its huge range and how it pops her up nicely for an actual combo (and eventually an uair to kill). If you shffl in you'll probably die which is not fun

If Samus is Above You

  • Sh uair works really well when she's above you, as well as bairing her landings per usual. Even if she got shield out, it would have set up a shield poke

Kill %

  • At kill %, you can kinda brazenly usmash her landings as all the bomb nonsense mainly serves to cancel your recovery if they hit you, and whether she missiles, nairs, or whatever doesn't really matter as they'll mostly result in her dying if you just call out when, as opposed to how she will
  • Weak nair breaks asdi down at 76%, which is fine since the main use is an usmash set up on samus WDing around anyways. Also if she's not near a ledge to ledge cancel it, it's often a mix up for her if she'll land on the platform or the ground. Usually they'll choose platform as landing on the ground risks an usmash, but since good foxes KNOW that they'll tend to land on the platform for that reason
