Fox vs Luigi

Wavedash mixups and edgeguarding

← Back to Matchups

Concepts (Mostly from Cookbook)

  • Imagine that luigi is a rook in chess; he cannot move horizontally and vertically at the same time
  • The floor is lava until we are at 50%
  • He has to wavedash to threaten, but if we full hop preemptively and retreat to top platform if they don't wavedash under us, we should be safe
  • At high %, aerials are scary to hit by, but down smash isn't (at least not until it kills)
  • Dair up tilt is good, Dair shine is pretty bad
  • Dair from 0-40% is strong
  • Nair from 40-70% is good
  • Up tilt is good at almost all %
  • Aerial→spot dodge might be better than aerial → shine
  • F throw → Fair may be better than Up throw → Shark
  • Up smash trades with luigi in air often
  • Wavedash oos punish vs dsmash
  • Roll on stage using the C stick or a soft press of the shield. Can prevent dying from getting hit by up B and air dodging
  • If rolling up vs luigi on edge we can input a shine during the roll frames in case up B hits us and we need to counter
  • Shine B moves on reaction
  • Marth killer vs side b, and shine if they are still off stage, or ledge dash and punish if they continue on stage

Additional Concepts from Siddward

  • Running shine / Double shine is useful similar to how it's useful vs IC's
  • Luigi will often use walling aerials to prevent us from doing certain things like approaching with full hop
  • Falling up air is very useful vs crouch
