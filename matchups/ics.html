Fox vs Ice Climbers

Separating climbers, Nana AI, and desyncs

← Back to Matchups

General Concepts

Nana States

  • Nana has 2 primary states of being: Connected and disconnected
  • Connected: Her inputs are coming from the player
  • Disconnected: The AI is taking over
  • Nana, when connected, gets the exact same inputs, just 6 frames later
  • Every frame that nana is connected, regardless of whether she is on the ground or in the air, she is acted on by a force that attracts her to popo's position
  • Nana is forced to look the same direction that popo was looking 5 frames prior
  • Desyncs are still in the connected state, the inputs just act on whoever is actionable
  • Nana cannot connect out of the teeter animation
  • Squall hammer (side B) and Belay (up B) force connection

Connection Requirements

  • To connect, 3 things must be true: Both climbers must be on the ground, Climbers must be within the connection distance, Climbers must have nearly equivalent momentum
  • Climbers can connect even on different platforms as long as they are grounded and nearby

Nana AI Behavior

  • Nana's CPU level is determined by popo's damage
  • When disconnected, the AI may go into a "chase state" where she just wants to get back to popo
  • If she reaches the spot, she waits for popo to land so she can reconnect
  • If she is in hitlag, she is disconnected
  • She will never attack while in recovery mode
  • She will always try to land on randall rather than the main stage
  • She has an attack mode where she can attack or dodge
  • She will only throw out a move if it is in range
  • At level 0: She pretty much just jabs
  • At level 3: She gains blizzard
  • At level 4: She gets aerials
  • At level 6 (~120%): She may even grab
  • If she's trying to wait to reconnect and an opponent shows up, she might swing
  • She cannot go straight to attack from hitlag or recovery

Strategy & Concepts

  • Ice climbers are weak against the outer edge of side platform
  • After a shine, we can get a running double shine
  • After they full hop up air, ICs have two primary options: Fair (Hard to space around with both hammers around, Will smack you if you try to run up CC the dair), Dair (Can be CC'd, Can't really whiff punish)
  • Between stocks, go to the far edge of a platform
  • Weird things happen with hitting their shield. It's possible to hit both shields at different times, causing double hitlag
  • Nana can buffer a frame perfect grab on shield by holding A during shield stun
  • Use drill when they aren't shielding, like whiff punishing fair after they use FH up air
  • Can full hop shine vs up air as a mixup to waiting for them to come down
  • Nana cannot fast fall or tech
  • If you up throw, popo is forced to double jump
  • It is best to throw them offstage if possible

Playstyles

  • There are 2 main styles of play: Full hop over them and shine, Up tilt / Get them in the air some how
  • If nana is recovering, short hop → shine → shinestall
  • Don't stand near Nana for too long; she may swing

Three Important Situational Goals

  • Fox pulls nana and kills her instantly
  • Fox gets popo alone and nana just stands around useless while you beat up the main climber
  • Fox combos nana and every time popo tries to save her, you hit him for it

Additional Tips

  • Look to edgeguard using full hop Bair
  • Falling up air is stronger than drill if they whiff
  • Don't try to dash dance punish. Being on the ground too much or too long is dangerous
  • FD and Stadium are strong for ICs because there is no top platform to escape to
  • We kind of have to camp sopo really hard
  • If they ledgedash, we must respect it and keep our distance, while pressuring them to approach
  • Use full hop to beat down smash / grab / other grounded options
  • Against up throw, we can slight DI behind and jump out
  • It is sometimes better to stay on a platform for a while than it is to switch platforms often
  • On battlefield if you JC shine nana offstage, waveland on platform → run off shine should catch nana's recovery
  • If popo is 0-20%, she will getup attack
  • Hold away during ledge handoffs to avoid throw f smash
  • If they are on platform, you can dash dance or get under them and up tilt
  • If they have to side B from below, nana can't grab edge, and neither can popo
  • Falling up air, especially on the back of their shield is really strong
  • Do not jump and land in center stage
  • On FD, stay grounded, use lots of shine
  • Never up tilt nana; popo will destroy us in many cases
  • Go to edge between stocks
  • Waveshine → walk → shine rather than dash JC shine because Popo can get us sometimes for the dash JC shine. Being caught without double jump is really bad
  • Popo is very likely to Fair while coming down
  • To edgehog Nana, either getup attack or roll
  • If you see blizzard, just wait
  • Neutral is largely about using shine to split them up effectively
