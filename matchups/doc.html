Matchup Philosophy - SFAT

Core Principles

  • Minimize interactions that you aren't in an advantage in
  • Doc cannot catch Fox - use your mobility advantage
  • Keep the punish simple into simple sharks - don't overcomplicate combos
  • Time your edgeguards correctly - this is key to winning the matchup

Things from Cookbook

  • Look out for Doc's Jab
  • Drill is a good approach
  • Spaced bair and up tilts can be hard for doc to beat
  • Using platforms can be hard for him to deal with
  • Nothing is guaranteed off of up throw but it's still worth it
  • Edgeguard is annoying, but possible
  • Can try to marth killer the pills and take edge
  • Can bair through the pills if he's recovering close
  • Don't wall ride up B; go straight up to avoid dying from cape
