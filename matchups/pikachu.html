Cookbook Concepts

  • Up throw single hit up air is the most reliable kill setup
  • Weak nair/bair up smash is very useful as well
  • Pika can double zip to ledge to avoid juggles when they are falling near the edge
  • Pikachu has no drift after his up B if he goes straight up, just like luigi
  • If you drill pikachu, grab and up tilt are both good options
  • Pika up air is very laggy if done low to the ground, but is also their best combo starter
  • Running shine → drill is good at low %
  • You can shield poke pikachu's tail fairly consistently
  • You can mash SDI up on pikachu down smash to get a reversal
  • Pikachu's options behind their shield are stronger than in front
