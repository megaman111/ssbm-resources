# SSBM Resources Hub

A comprehensive resource collection for Super Smash Bros. Melee players, featuring The Way of Fox matchup guides, doubles strategies, the Fox Cookbook, and curated content from top players.

## About

This site serves as a central hub for Melee resources, with a focus on Fox gameplay. It includes:

### The Way of Fox
A compilation of Fox knowledge from:
- **Fox Deconstructed** (Leffen)
- **Fiction's coaching** and analysis
- **Billybopeep's** insights
- **The Fox Cookbook**
- **Druggedfox** lessons
- Community knowledge from top players

### Other Resources
- **mmunder Comprehensive Doubles Bible** - Team strategies and fundamentals
- **Leffen's Fox Deconstructed** - Video series breakdown
- **Fiction's Coaching** - Advanced concepts
- **The Fox Cookbook** - Visual technique guides

## Features

- **Main Resource Hub**: Central page linking to all Melee resources
- **Matchup Guides**: Detailed breakdowns for all Fox matchups organized by Neutral → Punish → Edgeguarding → Defense → Stage-Specific
- **Doubles Guide**: Comprehensive team strategies from RunRiot, NMW, Zuppy, darkatma, and Zodd
- **Fox Cookbook**: Visual guides with video examples
- **Searchable Content**: Find specific techniques and concepts quickly
